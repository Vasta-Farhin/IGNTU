our facilities

Indira Gandhi National Tribal University, Amarkantak provides several infrastructural facilities for the students and staff.

central library

IGNTU has a well-equipped central library for the students and staff with a varied collection of books, resources, databases, etc.

sport complex

IGNTU has Indoor Sports Complex and Outdoor Ground include Four Pavilions for Cricket fans, Basket Ball, Football and Volley Ball Ground.

cafeteria

The cafeteria is commonplace with full of hygiene for the students to hang out. It offers a variety of cuisine, beverages and snacks.
